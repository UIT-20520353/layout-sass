East Village Ice Cream Crawl

We will stop at five different world-class ice cream shops on this 1.5 mile, 1.5-hour tour. At each ice cream store, we'll explore the story behind the business and see what makes the ice cream unique, as you savor every delightful flavor. These renowned ice cream parlors have a rich history of crafting the most mouthwatering frozen treats, each with its own secret recipes and techniques passed down through generations. From traditional classics to innovative and exotic creations, you'll embark on a delectable journey that delights the taste buds and leaves you with unforgettable memories. Whether you're a connoisseur of creamy goodness or simply enjoy indulging in sweet delights, this tour promises to be a delightful and enriching experience for all ice cream enthusiasts.
